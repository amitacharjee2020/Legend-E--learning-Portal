5. Write a letter to your younger brother advising him about the bad effects of wasting time. 20, Monipur, Dhaka. 14 June 2013 Dear Shorno, At first, take my heartiest love. Yesterday I received your a letter from mother and heard that you are wasting your valuable time in playing games, chatting in Facebook and mixing with some wicked boys. Let me tell you about the bad effects or wasting valuable time. You know that time and time wait for none . Again, youth is the seed time of time. The success in life totally depends on the proper use of youth that means student life. If you waste your time in this way, you will not be able to succeed in life. Besides, your concept about reality is very low. Life is not a bed of roses. You will have to struggle very hard to maintain yourself and your family. Without ensuring the proper use of time, it is impossible for everyone to be successful in life. All the great men of the world became successful only by using their time properly. You must keep it in mind that time once is gone, is gone forever. I believe you will understand it by now and make the proper use of your valuable time in study. No more today. Convey my best regards to mom and love to the youngers.
